About Me

I make Web3 make sense

I’m Andy Joe. I love taking messy, complex Web3 ideas and turning them into content people can digest and act on. With a background in Computer Science, I naturally think in systems and logic, which helps me explain DeFi mechanisms, protocols, and tokenomics with clear details. My goal is to help crypto and blockchain teams communicate their value effectively.
